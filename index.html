📌 Admission Process 2026
📝 Step 1: Apply Online ▾

Candidates must register on the official Amity admission portal by entering details such as name, email ID, mobile number, and preferred program. The registered email and mobile are verified through OTP, which activates the application account.

Steps to Apply:
  • Visit portal.amity.edu and create a new account
  • Fill in personal details, academic qualifications & course preferences
  • Upload scanned copies of Class 10 & 12 mark sheets, ID proof, photograph & certificates
  • Pay the non-refundable application fee of ₹1,500 via debit card, credit card, net banking or UPI
  • Submit the form and note down the application reference number

💡 Offline option: Visit the campus admission office to collect and submit a physical application form.

🎯 Step 2: Selection Round ▾

Shortlisting is based on a combination of entrance exam scores, academic performance, and a personal interview. Depending on the program, candidates may appear for national-level exams or the university's own AMCAT test.

Selection Process:
  • Appear for the required entrance exam — JEE Main (B.Tech), CAT/MAT (MBA), CUET UG (UG programs), CLAT (Law), NATA (B.Arch) or AMCAT (university-level test)
  • Candidates with valid scores in accepted national exams may be exempted from AMCAT
  • For merit-based direct admission — 80%+ in Class XII (or 70%+ percentile in CAT/MAT for MBA)
  • Shortlisted candidates are called for a Personal Interview (or Skype interview for out-of-state candidates)
✅ Step 3: Confirm Admission ▾

Selected candidates receive an admission offer letter on the portal, via email and SMS. The offer includes details such as semester fee, course start date, venue, hostel information, scholarship (if applicable), and the list of documents required for enrolment.

To Confirm Your Seat:
  • Pay the initial semester fee within the stipulated time to confirm admission
  • Failure to pay within the deadline results in automatic cancellation of the offer
  • Visit the campus on the reporting date with original documents for verification
  • Documents required: Class 10 & 12 mark sheets, graduation marksheets (for PG), entrance exam scorecard, ID proof, transfer certificate, and passport-size photographs
🎓

Direct Admission (Merit-based)

Candidates with 80%+ aggregate in Class XII (CBSE/ISC/State Boards) are eligible for direct admission — no entrance exam required. For MBA, CAT/MAT 70+ percentile candidates get direct GD/PI call.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Amity University Jaipur UGC approved?
Yes, Amity University Jaipur is recognized by UGC (University Grants Commission) and approved by AICTE, BCI, COA & other statutory bodies. The university holds NAAC A+ accreditation.
What is the eligibility for B.Tech at Amity Jaipur?
Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics (PCM) with minimum 50% aggregate. Valid score in JEE Main/CUET-UG or AMCAT test is required. Admission is also based on academic merit.
What is the fee for MBA program at Amity Jaipur?
The 1st year fee for MBA at Amity University Jaipur is approximately ₹4.24 Lakhs. Eligibility: Graduation with minimum 50% marks plus valid CAT/MAT/XAT/NMAT/GMAT score. Scholarships are available for meritorious students.
Does Amity Jaipur provide hostel facilities?
Yes, Amity University Jaipur is a fully residential campus with separate hostels for boys and girls. The campus has 3,100+ hostel capacity with 24x7 security, Wi-Fi, mess, laundry, gym & medical facilities available.
What is the placement record of Amity Jaipur?
Amity Jaipur has an excellent placement record with the highest package of ₹30 LPA and average package of ₹6.5 LPA. Over 1,200 students placed in 2024 with 100+ recruiters including Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Deloitte & more.
Can I apply without an entrance exam?
Yes, for many programs, admission is merit-based. Candidates with 80%+ aggregate in Class XII (CBSE/ISC/State Boards) may be exempt from the selection process. However, programs like B.Tech (JEE/CUET) and MBA (CAT/MAT) require valid entrance scores.
What programs are available at Amity Jaipur?
Amity University Jaipur offers 300+ programs across Engineering (B.Tech, M.Tech), Management (BBA, MBA), Law (BA LLB, BBA LLB, LLM), Design (B.Des, M.Des), Architecture (B.Arch), Science (B.Sc, M.Sc), Commerce (B.Com, M.Com), Computer Applications (BCA, MCA), Pharmacy, Physiotherapy, Biotechnology, Forensic Sciences, Psychology, Hotel Management, and many more at UG, PG, Diploma & Doctoral levels.
Is Amity University Jaipur degree valid for higher education abroad?
Yes, Amity degrees are recognized by UGC and are widely accepted for higher education in India and abroad. Many alumni have pursued Master's and PhD programs at leading international universities. The degree is eligible for equivalency assessments by international evaluation agencies.
